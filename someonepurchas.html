Product Purchase Popup Notification

Description:

  • A popup notification appears on your Shopify store every 15 seconds, indicating that someone has just purchased a product. This notification is designed to display for all the products in your store sequentially.

Purpose:

Create Urgency:

  • By showing that products are being purchased frequently, it encourages visitors to make a purchase before the item goes out of stock.

Build Social Proof:

  • Seeing that other customers are buying products can increase trust and influence potential buyers' decisions.

Appearance:

The popup appears at the bottom right corner of the screen, regardless of the device being used (desktop, tablet, or mobile). Each notification includes:

  • A small image of the product.

  • The text: "Someone purchased this product now."

Example:

  • On different devices (laptop, tablet, mobile), the popup looks consistent and is positioned at the bottom right corner. The attached image showcases how this popup appears across different devices.

Interval:

  • The popup changes every 15 seconds to show a new product from your store, ensuring that all products are featured over time.

This setup can significantly enhance the user experience by adding dynamic elements to your store and leveraging psychological triggers to boost sales.
