etting Up a Bundle Discount Collection in Shopify

Step 1: Create a Collection

Log in to Shopify Admin Panel:

  • Go to your Shopify admin dashboard.

Create a New Collection:

  • Navigate to Products > Collections > Create Collection.

  • Name your collection (e.g., "Bundle Products").

  • Set the collection type to Manual or Automated based on your preference. For this setup, we’ll use Manual.

  • Add products to the collection by selecting them from your inventory.

Step 2: Add Products to the Collection

Select Products:

  • Go to the newly created collection.

  • Click on Add Products.

  • Select the products you want to include in the bundle discount and click Add.

Step 3: Create the Discount

Navigate to Discounts:

  • Go to Discounts in the Shopify admin panel.

  • Click on Create Discount.

Set Discount Details:

  • Choose Automatic Discount.

  • Name your discount (e.g., "Bundle Discount").

  • Set the Discount Type to Percentage and enter "15%" as the discount amount.

  • In the Applies To section, select Specific Collections and choose your "Bundle Products" collection.

Set Minimum Requirements:

  • Under Minimum Requirements, choose Minimum Quantity of Items.

  • Set the quantity to 3, meaning customers must add at least three items from the collection to get the discount.

Active Dates:

  • Set the Start Date and, if needed, an End Date for the discount.

  • Click Save Discount.

Step 4: Customize the Online Store (Optional)

Theme Customization:

  • Go to Online Store > Themes > Customize.

  • You may want to create a dedicated section on your homepage or a specific page to promote the bundle discount.

  • Use the theme editor to add a banner or a section that highlights the bundle discount.

Promotional Content:

  • Create promotional content or a popup that informs customers about the 15% discount on the bundle.

  • Use marketing tools or apps to ensure customers are aware of the offer.

Step 5: Test the Setup

Add Products to Cart:

  • Visit your online store as a customer.

  • Add three products from the "Bundle Products" collection to the cart.

Verify Discount:

  • Go to the cart and check if the 15% discount is applied correctly.

  • Ensure the discount is visible and the final price reflects the discount.

Expected Output

  • When customers visit your store, they can see the "Bundle Products" collection.
  • If they add at least three products from this collection to their cart, a 15% discount will automatically apply.
  • The discount will be visible in the cart summary, showing the original price, discount amount, and final price after the discount.

This setup ensures that customers are incentivized to buy more products, increasing your average order value while providing a seamless shopping experience.
